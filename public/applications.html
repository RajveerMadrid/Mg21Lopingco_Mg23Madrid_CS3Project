Reaction Time Test

This reaction time test will measure your reaction time using a visual stimulus. Click the screen when you see it turn green! Don't click too early as your attempt will be invalidated. The average reaction time of a human is around 250 milliseconds. Do you think you have what it takes to be faster? Well, find out by taking the test!
